Fun Facts About Laddus

Did you know? Laddus are said to have originated in ancient India, and they were originally made as energy snacks for warriors!

Laddus are often served at festivals, weddings, and special occasions. In fact, Motichoor Laddus are a favorite offering to Lord Ganesha during Ganesh Chaturthi.
